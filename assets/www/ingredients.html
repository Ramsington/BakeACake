Ingredients

  • 200g good quality dark chocolate
  • 200g butter
  • 85g self-raising flour
  • 85g plain flour
  • 1/4 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 200g light muscovado sugar
  • 200g golden caster sugar
  • 25g cocoa powder
  • 3 medium eggs
  • grated chocolate or curls, to decorate

Method

  1. Pre-heat Oven to 140/160/Gas Mark 3
  2. Butter cake tin
  3. Break the chocolate and butter into pieces and put into the pan
  4. Warm pan over low heat until melted
  5. Sieve the flour, bicarbonate, sugar and cocoa powder into a bowl
  6. Break eggs into bowl adn Stir
  7. Pour melted chocolate mix and egg mix into flour mix
  8. stir until well blended
  9. Pour into cake tin
  10. Cook for around 1 hour 25-30 minutes
